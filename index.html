My Story

I am a Master's candidate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University School of Medicine (SJTUSM, Top 1 medical school in China). My specialization is in ophthalmology, and I hold a Bachelor's degree in clinical medicine from SJTUSM as well. As a graduate researcher, i mainly focus on uncovering the mechanism of retinal aging and retinal degeneration. My current research interests revolve around the intricate interplay of mitochondria, aging, and metabolism. I also serve as a ophthalmologist for Shanghai General hospital. I collaborate with optical engineers to bridge the gap between optical technology and clinical needs, with a focus on applications like blue light in infectious corneal disease and Raman spectral imaging in slit lamps.

Off the white coat, I am...

Amateur Violinist

I embarked on my musical journey at the age of 7, learning the violin under the guidance of Prof. Jiamin Yuan from the prestigious Shanghai Conservatory of Music . For nearly a decade, Prof. Yuan has not only been my mentor but also a cherished friend who continues to inspire my passion for music.

CRAZY about classical music

From 2008 to 2015, I was a violist in the Pudong Youth Symphony Orchestra, where my love for classical music blossomed. We were so lucky to have the beloved Prof. Ji Ruikai (Central Conservatory of Music) as our conductor. The resonance of each note, the harmony of every instrument, and the profound emotion of classical compositions have since become an inseparable part of my life's soundtrack, continually inspiring and enriching my journey.

Memorable performance experience

During my time in the symphony orchestra, we were exceptionally fortunate to perform at some of the world's most iconic venues, including the Sydney Opera House, Shanghai Concert Hall, Shanghai Oriental Art Center, and the renowned Shanghai Music Festival. These unforgettable performances allowed me to not only share the beauty of classical music with diverse audiences but also to be deeply moved by the profound connection that music creates across cultures and continents.

Second chapter of musical exploration

Spanning from 2015 to 2020, I embarked on a new adventure as a violist with the Student Symphony Orchestra at SJTU.

The nightingale continues to sing...

From 2022 to the present day, i joined forces with two talented musicians, brought together through the serendipity of the internet. Together, playing as a trio, we play our music not only in practice rooms but also in the joyous celebrations of people's weddings.

Sports

My fitness journey began as a quest to fortify myself for the rigorous experiments and demanding life of graduate studies. I've explored a multitude of sports genres, ranging from bodycombat and battlewave to bodypump, weight-lifting, and more. Along this athletic journey, I've had the privilege to train with a spectrum of coaches and engage with vibrant communities. These experiences have not only enhanced my physical fitness but also cultivated mental resilience, representing a holistic journey of self-improvement.
